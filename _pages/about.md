Before that, I received my PhD degree at [Fuzhou University](https://ccds.fzu.edu.cn/), supervised by [Prof. Wenzhong Guo](https://ccds.fzu.edu.cn/info/1202/4993.htm) and [Prof. Shiping Wang](https://ccds.fzu.edu.cn/info/1202/8958.htm). From 2021.10-2022.10, I am a visiting student in the School of Data Science, Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen), China, supervised by [Prof. Jicong Fan](https://jicongfan.github.io/). From 2023.01-2023.06, I am a visiting student in the Cooperative AI Lab, King's College London, UK, supervised by [Prof. Yali Du](https://yalidu.github.io/).
